---
name: npc-forge
description: Create a memorable NPC tied to current loose ends
---

Read `world.md`, `roster.md`, and the most recent session's "Loose ends".
Invent one NPC who complicates an open hook: distinct voice, a want, a secret,
a reason the party can't ignore them. Create them as `npcs/<slug>.npc.md`
using the NPC frontmatter shape, and add a one-line entry to `roster.md`.
